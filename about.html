Welcome to our online store!

We are a local business based in Maribor, dedicated to bringing the joy of gaming to people of all ages. Our collection includes some of the most popular and timeless games, including sliding puzzle, monopoly, and chess. We believe that games have the power to bring people together, spark creativity, and provide a welcome escape from the stresses of everyday life.


Sliding puzzle: Sliding puzzle is a classic game that challenges players to rearrange a set of tiles to form a complete picture. The tiles slide along the board and can only be moved into an empty space, so players must think strategically to solve the puzzle.


Monopoly: Monopoly is a classic board game that has been enjoyed by families for generations. Players roll the dice to move around the board, buying and selling properties and collecting rent from other players. The goal is to become the wealthiest player and bankrupt your opponents.


Chess: Chess is a timeless game of strategy that has been played for over a thousand years. It is played on a board with 64 squares, and each player controls a set of pieces with different abilities. The goal is to capture the opponent's king while protecting your own. Chess requires patience, focus, and strategic thinking, making it a great mental exercise for players of all ages.
